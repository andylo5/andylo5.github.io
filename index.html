About the Attorney

Andrés Santiago Lozano, Esq.

'The courthouse is a strange land where they speak a strange language. My job is to be your translator, your guide, and your fiercest advocate every step of the way.'

Andrés believes that every person deserves skilled, attentive legal representation regardless of the complexity of their case or the pressure they're under. With a practice rooted in Chattanooga, Tennessee, he brings a personal, client-first approach to every case.

Whether you're facing criminal charges or navigating a difficult family transition, Andrés is here to listen, advise, and advocate fiercely on your behalf. You'll always have direct access — no hand-offs to junior associates.
